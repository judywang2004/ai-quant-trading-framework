7:Future AI Integration
Strategy

↓

Signal

↓

↓

The AI does not replace the strategy. It evaluates the signal generated by the rule-based logic and can adjust confidence or reject low-quality trades. This keeps the system explainable and makes the AI an enhancement rather than the core decision-maker.

8: Design Principles

I’d like us to adopt these engineering principles from day one:

1. Single Responsibility Principle – Each module has one clear job.
2. Strategy Independence – Strategies should not depend on MT5, TradingView, or any broker API.
3. Pluggable Components – Risk managers, indicators, and executors can be swapped without rewriting strategies.
4. Testability – Every module should be unit-testable in isolation.
5. Research First – Every strategy is validated through backtesting before it is considered for live execution.
